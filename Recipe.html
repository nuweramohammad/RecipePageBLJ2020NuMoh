Step 1

Have all your ingredients ready. Start

Step 2

Blitz the butter, flour, sugar, vanilla in a food processor until the mixture resembles fine breadcrumbs.

Step 3

Add the egg yolk and blend until balls of dough have formed.

Step 4

Knead briefly to make a smooth ball.
Then let it sit for 30 minutes

Step 5

Lightly flour your work surface and roll out the dough.

Step 6

Use a 6cm star cutter to stamp out stars and transfer to a baking parchment

Step 7

Make sure that you make pairs!

Step 8

Leave the pairs in the oven for 15 minutes
On 180 Celcius

Last Step

Fill the space with jam, then sandwich a biscuit
Make sure that the cookie monster does not get you!
